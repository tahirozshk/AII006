Technical Architecture

In addition to UI design, I follow a strict MVC (Model-View-Controller) architecture, especially when working with Laravel. This decision is based on:

  • Separation of Concerns: Keeping business logic (Models) away from the user interface (Views).
  • Scalability: Allowing the application to grow without becoming a "spaghetti code" mess.
  • Security: Utilizing Laravel's built-in protections against SQL injection and XSS through its Eloquent ORM and Blade engine.

Visual Philosophy

For my initial projects, I am focusing on Minimalist Utility. I believe that a website's primary goal is to deliver information clearly and efficiently. My design decisions revolve around:

  • High Contrast: Ensuring readability through careful color selection.
  • Whitespace: Using breathing room to guide the user's eye and reduce cognitive load.
  • Responsive First: Building layouts that feel natural on both mobile and desktop screens.
